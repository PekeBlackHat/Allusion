# Privacy Policy

Allusion is designed around a simple principle:

> Public information only.

## What Allusion Accesses

* Public webpages
* Public repositories
* Public documentation
* Public research papers

## What Allusion Does Not Access

* Emails
* Contacts
* Calendars
* Private files
* Private cloud storage
* Private databases
* Social media accounts
* Content behind logins

## Authentication

Allusion does not collect credentials.

Allusion does not store passwords.

Allusion does not attempt to bypass authentication systems.

## Data Storage

Reports generated by Allusion are stored locally on the user's machine.

No report data is transmitted to external services beyond normal web requests required to access public webpages.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that any data collected complies with applicable laws, website terms of service, and organizational policies.

## Summary

Allusion is a public-web research tool.

It is intentionally designed to avoid access to private information.
